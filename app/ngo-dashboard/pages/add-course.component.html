What are the training courses for?

  • The training course provided by an NGO will be provided to the user who registers for our STEP programme
  • These courses are primarily for women who want to start earning from themselves and work independently to support their family and their future
  • The courses provided by an NGO will act as a skill development for all the hard working women.

What are the basic requirements for adding a course?

  • A training course option should be provided by NGO among the three:
    • Agriculture
    • Tailoring
    • Stitching
  • Every training course provided should have a Start date, End date along with the duration of the training
