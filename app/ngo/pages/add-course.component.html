What are the training courses for?

  • The training course provided by an NGO will be provided to the user who registers for our STEP programme
  • These courses are primarily for women who want to start earning from themselves and work independently to support their family and their future
  • The courses provided by an NGO will act as a skill development for all the hard working women.

Why should we register?

  • In principle, an NGO does not have to register itself to perform charitable, welfare or developmental activities.
  • There are some specific types of activities that can only be carried out if the NGO is registered under the country's specific acts or laws governing NGOs (particularly related to fund-raising).
  • Due to obligations required of a registered NGO, the registration process leads to the development of systematic thinking and functioning of NGOs.
